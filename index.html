Frequently Ask Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive HR consulting, training solutions, recruitment services, and capacity building programs tailored to your organization's needs.

How can I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by contacting us through our contact form, email, or phone. Our team will respond within 24 hours to discuss your requirements.

Where are you located?

We are based in Kenya with the capability to serve clients nationwide through both in-person and virtual solutions.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including corporate, public sector, NGOs, and educational institutions, providing customized HR solutions for each sector.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money (M-PESA), and corporate checks. We can also accommodate other payment arrangements based on your organization's requirements.

How long are your training programs?

Our training programs range from one-day workshops to comprehensive multi-week courses. The duration is tailored to your organization's needs and the complexity of the subject matter.

Do you offer customized training solutions?

Yes, we specialize in developing customized training programs that address your specific organizational needs and objectives. Our team works closely with you to create targeted solutions.

Can you conduct training at our premises?

Yes, we offer both on-site training at your location and off-site training at our facilities. We also provide virtual training options for remote teams and organizations.
